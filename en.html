Benefits

Mealworms are an excellent source of protein for your animals.

Thanks to our high protein content and healthy feeding protocols, they play a significant role in the healthy growth and development of your animals.

Moreover, mealworms are easily digestible and offer a rich content of vitamins and minerals. These nutrients help to strengthen the general health of the animals and meet their optimal nutritional needs.

Products

Live Mealworm

Live mealworms provide a fresh and nutritious option for your animals. They contribute to healthy growth with their high protein content.

Dried Mealworm

Dried mealworms come with the advantage of long storage while providing a healthy food source for your animals.

About Us

Hello, my name is Onur Can Mığırdağı. I am continuing my education in International Trade and Management at Yeditepe University. Since 2016, I have been engaged in mealworm breeding as a hobby. This hobby has led me to develop a sustainable social entrepreneurship project.

To support our project, we have conducted various experiments by applying a mealworm diet to chickens and fish during their growth and maturity periods. We were awarded the Jury Special Award in the "Dream It, Achieve It" competition. Additionally, we presented our project to professors from the University of Reading in England as part of a team that included Chinese and Indian students, and we carefully evaluated the feedback we received.

We continue to improve our work by constantly following up on current research. Within the next six months, we aim to reach the capacity to breed 20 million mealworms monthly. By achieving this goal, we aim to contribute to the animal husbandry sector, which is of great importance in our country, with our healthy and productivity-boosting feeds.
